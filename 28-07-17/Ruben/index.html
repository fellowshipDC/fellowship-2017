About

Questioning data and finding trends and answers are my passion, I do it trhough statistical packages like R, Stata and QGIS. Recently I am also learning to develop web pages in order to visualize and present data. I am a consultant with 10 years of experience in the professional services industry. I have participated in more than 50 projects in international public and private institutions advising governments, companies and civil society organizations. I am an economist from the University of the Americas Puebla and hold a Master of Science with a major in Public Policy and Administration from Carnegie Mellon University. I have worked as a consultant at the World Bank, Inter-American Development Bank, Deloitte, Evaluare Consultores, Victoria (Australia) Government, etc. I enjoy outdoors, rock climbing and cycling. I am a foody and love wine and beer tasting. I love to read about politics, new technologies, privacy and its ethical implications.
